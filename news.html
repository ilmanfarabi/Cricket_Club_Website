Tamim Iqbal was the leading run-scorer in BPL 2024

Runs 492, Average 35.14, Strike rate 127.13 Tamim rose to the top of this season's runs chart with his consistent showing for Barishal. Having made three fifties in the tournament - 71 against Durdanto Dhaka, 66 against Comilla and 52 not out against Chattogram Challengers - he struck a quick 39 in the final to lead Barishal to their maiden title. This was the fourth time he finished with 400-plus runs in a BPL season.
